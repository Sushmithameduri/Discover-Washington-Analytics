## üìä Analytics Dashboards & Performance


### Key Performance Outcomes (Phase 3 Growth)
* **Active User Growth:** +82.4% (reached 31 baseline users in 7 days)
* **Engagement Health:** Maintained an optimal **38% Bounce Rate** alongside an exceptional **8 min 59 sec Average Time on Site**, proving high content relevance.
* **Event Tracking:** Successfully recorded 946 digital behavioral events, isolating high-value user interactions.

## üí° Actionable Insights & Strategic Recommendations
By mining the GA4 data, I translated raw traffic metrics into clear solutions and content recommendations:

* **Traffic Forecasting & Publishing Strategy:** * *Data:* Traffic heavily peaked on Thursday, Saturday, and Sunday. 
  * *Action:* Adjusted the content calendar to publish new assets on Wednesdays, ensuring pages were indexed and available just before the weekend traffic surge.
* **Platform Optimization Focus:**
  * *Data:* 100% of users accessed the site via Desktop, with Chrome (~80%) as the dominant browser. 
  * *Action:* Recommended suspending mobile-optimization resources to prioritize high-fidelity desktop UI enhancements.
* **Content Media Performance:**
  * *Data:* "Chihuly Garden and Glass" (65 views, 123 events) significantly outperformed legacy national park content.
  * *Action:* Validated the business need to pivot editorial focus toward localized, high-density urban attractions to drive higher event interaction.
